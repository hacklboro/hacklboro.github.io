What is a Hackathon
A Hackathon is a 24h-long (or more) collaborative and creative development session, where teams (or individuals) develop a piece of software or hardware. At the end of the 24h of coding the teams will present their creation to the judges and several winners may arise - typically the leaders in each category. Examples are 'Best Ethical/For Good Hack' or 'Best Andriod App'
Why do Hackathons
Innovate! 24h hours worth of Hackathon can generate some cool stuff!
Meet some cool people!
Make connections with possible employers
It is an awesome experience, you get to do awesome work and its free!
