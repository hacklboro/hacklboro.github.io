Who are we?

Howdy, we are HackLboro! Loughborough Univerity’s first Hackathon.

What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is a collaborative and creative event. Where the participants create a piece of software or hardware and compete to win awards - for example ‘Most Ethical Hack’ or ‘Best Android App Hack’.

How often does this happen?

Well that is a big part of the Hackathon fun! They aren’t a monthly occurrence, or a year-long coursework-styled piece. Au contraire; a Hackathon is just 24 hours long. Testing not only the technical knowledge of the participants but their time-management and social abilities.
