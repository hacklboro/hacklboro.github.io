hack{Lboro}

Day Plan

March 12th 10AM: Log in

Get ready for the start of HackLboro! Participants and organisers must open the Discord channel.

March 12th 10.15AM: Opening Ceremony

Presentations, safety briefing and online etiquette.

March 12th 10.45AM: Theme Introduction

Presentation of the Theme and discussion on problem statements.

March 12th 11.30AM: Coding Starts

Teams use their Discord channel or other communication platform to develop their software system.

March 12th 09.00PM: AmongUs Social

This is a chance for participants to unwind and socialise with other teams.

March 12th 12.00PM: Closing of Day 1

Participants are encouraged to stop coding and get some sleep to continue working the following day.

March 13th 09.00AM: Enterprise Workshop

The workshop will address a range of topics from pitching to business startups.

March 13th 03.00PM: Zumba Fitness Session

Participants can get involved in this session to relax and stretch their bodies. Organiser: BollyARKK

March 13th 04.00PM: 2vs2 Coding Battle

This is a coding challenge in which teams of 2 participants can compete against each other.

March 13th 06.00PM: Submission Deadline

The final version of the systems must be on GitHub by this date and time.

March 13th 06.30PM: Closing of Day 2

The final version of the systems must be on GitHub by this date and time.

March 14th 09.30AM: Demos

Participants will walk the judges through their system in a live presentation.

March 14th 11.00AM: Award Ceremony and Closing of Day 2

Prizes given to winning teams and closing ceremony.
