Day Plan

March 12th 10:00AM

START OF HACKLBORO

Get ready for the Loughborough's Official Hackathon! Get logged in and setup. Participants and organisers must open the Discord channel.

March 12th 10:15AM:

Opening Ceremony

Presentations, safety briefing and online etiquette. Join the general channel.

March 12th 10:45AM:

Theme Introduction

Presentation of the theme and discussion on problem statements.

March 12th 11:30AM:

Coding Starts

Teams use their Discord channel or other communication platform to develop their software system.

March 12th 05:00PM:

Workshop by LSU Enterprise

This is a chance to learn how to pitch a business/startup 

March 12th 09:00PM:

Among Us Social

This is a chance for participants to unwind and socialise with other teams.

March 12th 10:00PM:

Late Night Push

Participants can continue working on their project with their team. We recommend not working later than midnight.

March 13th 09:00AM:

Morning Ice-breaker

Start of day 2: Coding Battle 2V2 – join & earn more points!

March 13th 03:00PM:

Zumba Fitness Session

Participants can get involved in this session to relax and stretch their bodies.

Organiser: BollyARKK

March 13th 06:00PM:

Submission Deadline

The final version of the systems must be on GitHub by this date and time.

March 13th 06:30PM:

Closing of Day 2

Use the rest of the evening to prepare for the demo in the morning!

March 14th 09:00AM:

Prepare for demo

Participants should meet with their team and prepare for their demos

March 14th 09:30AM:

Demos

Participants will walk the judges through their system in a live presentation.

March 14th 10:45AM:

End Of Demos

Whilst the judges choose their winners, Hackathons UK will run their version of Pictionary!

March 14th 11:30AM:

Award Ceremony and Closing of Day 2

Prizes given to winning team! Closing ceremony.

March 14th 12:30PM:

END OF HACKLBORO







Please be advised that the day plan is not finalised and can change without prior notice.
