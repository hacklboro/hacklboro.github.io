Day Plan

March 12th 10AM:

START OF HACKLBORO

Get ready for the Loughborough's Official Hackathon! Get logged in and setup. Participants and organisers must open the Discord channel.

March 12th 10.15AM:

Opening Ceremony

Presentations, safety briefing and online etiquette.

March 12th 10.45AM:

Theme Introduction

Presentation of the theme and discussion on problem statements.

March 12th 11.30AM:

Coding Starts

Teams use their Discord channel or other communication platform to develop their software system.

March 12th 09.00PM:

Among Us Social

This is a chance for participants to unwind and socialise with other teams.

March 12th 10.00PM:

Late Night Push

Participants can continue working on their project with their team. We recommend not working later than midnight.

March 13th 09.00AM:

Enterprise Workshop

The workshop will address a range of topics from pitching to business startups.

March 13th 03.00PM:

Zumba Fitness Session

Participants can get involved in this session to relax and stretch their bodies.

Organiser: BollyARKK

March 13th 04.00PM:

2v2 Coding Battle

This is a coding challenge in which teams of 2 participants can compete against each other.

March 13th 06.00PM:

Submission Deadline

The final version of the systems must be on GitHub by this date and time.

March 13th 06.30PM:

Closing of Day 2

Relax and take the evening off.

March 14th 09.00AM:

Prepare for demo

Participants should meet with their team and prepare for their demos

March 14th 09.30AM:

Demos

Participants will walk the judges through their system in a live presentation.

March 14th 10.30AM:

End Of Demos

Participants can take a well-earned break! The judges will choose their winner

March 14th 11.00AM:

Award Ceremony and Closing of Day 2

Prizes given to winning teams and closing ceremony.

March 14th 12.00PM:

END OF HACKLBORO
